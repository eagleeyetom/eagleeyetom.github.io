about: |
  <p><strong>Baltazar Theatre</strong>, founded in 1998, is the only professional theatre in Hungary where all actors are people with intellectual disabilities. The group has become a significant part of Hungarian theatrical life, breaking new ground by showcasing actors' talents without focusing on their disabilities.</p>
  <p>From its inception, Baltazar Theatre's purpose has been to bring the non-disabled and disabled parts of society closer together. Years of artistic work have proven that the word "disability" means nothing when it comes to art.</p>
  <p>The ensemble is led by directors-choreographers Dorka Farkas and Olívia Réti-Harmath, along with writer István Vörös. The cast includes performers such as Balázs Erdős, Dániel Fehér, Szilvia Horváth, Anna Keresztes, Zsófia Kocsi, and many other dedicated artists.</p>
