about: |
  <p><strong>MTT Musical Theatre Troupe</strong> is a Lithuanian theatre ensemble operating under the
                          Lithuanian Association of Disabled Persons. MTT brings together professional actors,
                          professional musicians, and a very sensitive social group – people with disabilities – creating
                          a unique space for integrated artistic expression.</p>
  <p>The ensemble builds performances based on body expression, rhythm, and organic narrative
                          structures. MTT's creative practices are rooted in experimentation and collective search for a
                          scenic language that transcends verbal forms of communication.</p>
  <p>MTT has participated in international festivals in <strong>Latvia, Poland, Ukraine, and
                              Belarus</strong>, where they have won awards and recognition from audiences and theatre
                          critics. Central to their work is the principle of <em>open composition</em> – each performance
                          evolves through a process of collective improvisation and movement documentation.</p>
history: |
  <p>MTT Musical Theatre Troupe was founded as an educational-artistic project under the Lithuanian
                          Association of Disabled Persons. From the beginning, the ensemble focused on creating space for
                          artistic expression for people with diverse physical and cognitive experiences.</p>
  <p>The performance <em>Bioforms. Identification.</em> was created under the direction of Vitalij
                          Ljubota, a Ukrainian artist collaborating with the ensemble. Work on the performance was
                          completed in February 2022 – just before the outbreak of war in Ukraine. This piece stands as
                          the director's last completed artistic project before the dramatic change in reality, giving it
                          special historical and emotional significance.</p>
