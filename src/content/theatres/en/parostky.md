about: |
  <p><strong>Folk Amateur Theatre – Studio PAROSTKI</strong> is a Ukrainian inclusive theatre ensemble
                          based in Kyiv. The group brings together people with diverse psychophysical and intellectual
                          development, creating a unique space for integrated artistic expression and rehabilitation
                          through theatre.</p>
  <p>The ensemble is directed by <strong>Sofia Nesterova</strong>, who collaborates closely with
                          artistic director <strong>Vitalij Ljubota</strong>. Their creative practices are rooted in
                          experimentation and collective search for a scenic language that transcends verbal forms of
                          communication.</p>
  <p>The performance <em>Saint Nicholas Night</em> was created and rehearsed under wartime conditions
                          following the outbreak of the full-scale war in Ukraine in 2022. Despite ongoing challenges, the
                          ensemble continued their work, demonstrating remarkable resilience and dedication to their
                          artistic mission.</p>
  <p>PAROSTKI has participated in international festivals and received recognition for their
                          innovative approach to inclusive theatre. Central to their work is the principle of
                          <em>creativity over pity</em> – each performance evolves through a process of collective
                          improvisation and attention to individual abilities.
                      </p>
