What has changed?

We analysed structure, accessibility and design to deliver a modernized experience. Obsolete tags were removed, headings were reorganized and inline styles were replaced with a clean external stylesheet. Responsive design ensures the festivals story looks great on every screen.

  • Accessibility controls

    Toggle high-contrast mode, adjust text size, and rely on Local Storage to keep your preferences.

  • Mobile-first layout

    Optimized breakpoints and fluid typography seamlessly adapt from phones to large screens.

  • Multilingual routing

    Quick language switching with URLs that respect the existing /pl/ and /en/ structure.

Modernization Roadmap

Stage 1 路 Audit

Stage 2 路 Redesign
