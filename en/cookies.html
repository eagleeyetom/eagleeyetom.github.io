Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 05/09/2023

This Cookie Policy describes how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website https://masska.org. This Policy also covers your choices and rights regarding cookies.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that store information on your device, such as a computer, tablet, or smartphone, when you visit a website. They can be used for various purposes, including analyzing website traffic, customizing content, remembering user preferences, and more.

2. What cookies do we use?

On our website, we use the following types of cookies:

  • Analytical cookies: We use cookies to collect information on how users use our website. These cookies help us analyze website traffic and improve its features.
  • Functional cookies: We use cookies that allow us to remember user preferences, such as the chosen website's visual style.

3. How to control cookies?

If you want to manage or disable cookies, you can do so through your web browser settings. Usually, in the browser options, you will find tools for managing, deleting, or disabling cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some elements of our website.

4. Google Analytics

On our website, we use Google Analytics, a tool that collects data about website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to gather this information. To learn more about this tool and its operation, visit https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245?hl=en.

5. Changes to the Cookie Policy

We reserve the right to update this Cookie Policy at any time. Any changes will be published on our website, so we encourage you to check this page regularly for the latest information.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Cookie Policy, please contact the organizer.

Thank you for using our website!
