§ 2 Types of Cookies

  • External cookies - files collected and read from the User's device through the external system of the website

  • Permanent cookies - files collected and read from the User's device through the internalor external websites until the moment of the manual removal. Files are not automatically deleted after closing the session until the user's cookie configuration is set differently.
