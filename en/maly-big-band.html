Little Big Band

Łódź

„20-year-olds - the ninth cloud ahead of us”

12 min.

This is the story of two travelers - a boy and a girl who meet at the station, where they both wait for the train. Initially, strangers grow to feel better together with each moment, discover many common topics for conversation, tell their stories, they like each other more and more ...

Two actors are accompanied by a music band that plays percussion arrangements for fragments of popular Polish songs.

The spectacle was created on the occasion of the jubilee of the "Little Big Band" Percussion Orchestra and was to show on the one hand the meaning of the passing time and continuous journey through life, but also how important it is to stop and get to know someone closer. This someone can become someone close in time.

Songs used in the performance tell about various, more or less important matters but everything is aimed at enjoying the moment and just have fun ...

  • Iwona Starkowska - Music instructor

"Little Big Band" is a team of people with intellectual disabilities - mainly with Down syndrome, playing on various, simple percussion instruments. It is mainly a music band, but it additionally uses various forms of artistic expression and prepares both instrumental and dance or theater presentations.
