Description of the theatre's activity:

The group consists of people with disabilities - participants of the Environmental House of Self-help in Odolanów. As part of its operation, theatrical-multimedia projects are prepared and presented during internal artistic performances, as well as in front of a wider audience, including: parents and caretakers, children and youth (including from the School Complex in Odolanów, the Secondary School in Odolanów), the local community and supra-local (e.g. from the area of ​​the Commune and Town of Odolanów, Ostrów County, Greater Poland Province).

The group presents its achievements every year during performances at Odolanów's "Integration" - Disabled People's Day, and has often presented itself at the "Masska" Theater Forms Review for Disabled Children and Youth. The group also took part in the National Festival of Independent Theatres OFTeN in Ostrów Wielkopolski and the Regional Presentations of Children's and Youth Theatres in Kalisz. It has also presented its theatrical-multimedia projects in front of lecturers and students of the University of Adam Mickiewicz in Poznań, Faculty of Pedagogy and Art in Kalisz. At a special invitation, it performed at the International Scientific Symposium "Art therapy in a multicultural space" in Kalisz. In 2016, it also pres Łódź.

Information about the spectacle:

The theatrical and multimedia project "... the depths of me" is an implementation built on the basis of the functioning of people with disabilities, which are the starting point for project implementation, and its entirety relates and can be understood in relation to each person, each of us, becoming a form of interaction and building emotional relations between actors and recipients. The performance is a search for what bothers us, what we worry about, what makes us wonder, what we are looking for, and above all how we find and understand our adulthood - the adulthood of a person with a disability. The design of the project reaches back to the classic theatre with the features of a pantomime game, a puppet theatre, combined with audiovisual elements. During the performance, in addition to the actors playing on the stage, film materials are complementing the entire production. The project is supplemented and filled with appropriate background music enriched with statements by people with disabilities, thematically related to the subsequent stages of the performance. The whole is surrounded and filled with properly selected stage lighting. Such a project is a multi-stage implementation, and building its final form is usually carried out over a period of several months, and carried out as part of therapeutic classes. The doll and the costumes were made by the participants under the supervision of the caretakers of the Community Home of Mutual Aid in Odolanów.
