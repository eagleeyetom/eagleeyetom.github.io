Integrated Theatre "Azdak's garden"

Tbilisi

Extracurricular lesson

112 min.

The Extracurricular Lesson is based on Lyudmila Razumovskaya's play "Dear Elena Sergeevna", which is a psychological drama and reflects the current problem of all times, violence. Four high school students wish their teacher a happy birthday at home, and after unsuccessfully pleading, they force him to return the test so they can write well and get high marks. And Elena Sergeevna refuses...

In March 2016, the integrated theater "Azdaki Baghi" was founded, where disabled and professional actors play on the same stage. The goal of the integrated theater is for people with disabilities who are interested in theater to try their hand at acting, stand next to professional actors, and earn an income from what they love, not a ridiculous pension from the state. All this leads to an increase in the degree of their independence, an increase in self-esteem and, most importantly, a radical change in life for the better. After the projects, the following plays remained in the theater's repertoire: "Gypsies", "New Year's Eve in the Fairy Tale", "is a man a human?!", "47=46", "Threepenny Opera", "Teleplay-extracurricular lesson" , "Sherekilebi", "Hellados". 20 people with disabilities and 20 professional actors are united in this theater, although over the years 200 people with disabilities and 100 artists have been involved in our projects..
