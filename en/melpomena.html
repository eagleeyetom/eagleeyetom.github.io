Description of the theatre:

The theatre operates in the Art Therapy Center, the actors are Disabled people (mostly autistic youth) and their parents. The Fairy Tale of the Blue Bird was the first performance prepared to be presented as a street project on the occasion of the World Autism Day and once again at the 2nd International Festival of Artists of the Untouched Trail in Przemyśl. We participated in the 21st meeting with the music of the Artists of the Untreated Glass Fairy Tales and Legends in Lublin and in the 11th Provincial Review of Patriotic Songs. Our modest repertoire includes the performance "And it all began in Bethlehem" and "Love" - a street project of the mystery of the Passion.

Description of the play:

The Fairy Tale of the Blue Bird is a story that will take us to a strange garden, where we will meet the wonderful Blue Bird. And he ... will take us by the hand and show us his secret world ... the world of autism ...
