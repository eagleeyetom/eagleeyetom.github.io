About the performance

"Scenario for Three Actors" is a comedy by Bogusław Schaeffer, in which a director and two actors attempt to rehearse their latest play. Each character has a distinct personality, yet all are narcissistic and egotistical in their own way, often creating tension among the protagonists. Many situations border on the absurd, as the artists blend visions with reality. They explore the very essence of theatre and the meaning of art, interweaving it with the prose of life.

About the ensemble

Otczapy Theatre operates within the Września Cultural Center. Founded in 2011, the ensemble has produced approximately ten performances since its inception.
