Description of the play:

In the beginning there were memories...

The smell of grandma's cake ... the taste of powdered orangeade ... gifts under the Christmas tree ... the voice of a mother reading a bedtime story ... touch of grandfather's warm, rough hands on his cheek ... old photos ...

On the one hand, our performance is a sentimental journey to the land of childhood, and on the other, it is an encounter with your inner child.

What do we keep in the recesses of our memory?

Why do we remember?

Why do we forget?

What do we want to remember?

What do we want to forget?

Let's remember together. Many childhood memories are shared by everyone, they are with us all life, whether we like it or not, and the good ones give a sense of security and even bliss.
