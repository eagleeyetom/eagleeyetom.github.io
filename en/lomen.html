Ło/Men Theatrical Group

Lubawa

Kwadrat / Square

40 min.

Kwadrat is a play in the style of absurd theater. "There is some eternal need for hierarchy and submission in human beings. Laugh a lot, but be careful whom you laugh with. You might not even notice when you turn into a clown, and the life of a clown is unstable..."

history

The Youth Theater Group Ło/Men has been active and creating since 2012 at the Municipal Cultural Center in Lubawa. It brings together high school students and anyone whose soul resonates with theater. The group meets regularly to hone their skills, participates in festivals and competitions, and gets involved in local activities to provide entertainment for children and residents of Lubawa. The group has already performed 7 plays and achieved many significant successes.
