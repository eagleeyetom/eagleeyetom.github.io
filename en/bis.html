Description of the theatre:

"BIS" Theatre has been operating with great passion for many years at SOSW No. 2 in Gdynia. A few years ago, we reached the adulthood and we have the artistic ID cards in the form of numerous awards and distinctions. Acting training, improvisation, interpretation of script texts have always been a tool for discovering the creative potential of my students. Through the spontaneous activity of creating a performance, we always get the effect of an extraordinary theatrical experience.

Summary of the play:

Self-adaptation of the short story "Love of neighbor". Our performance is a satire on human vices, showing human flaws in a mocking and ironic way. The head of a mountain chalet tries to win customers at all costs and in a dishonest way…. And how will it end…. We look forward to showing it to you!
