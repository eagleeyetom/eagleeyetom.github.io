Visage Movement Theatre "Lik"

Tashkent

THE INTERCESSION

90 min.

The performance is dedicated to Holy Russia, which was so called not because everyone was holy, but because holiness was the ideal of the people. The play reflects the joyful and tragic pages of the history of Russia and how in different circumstances people aspired to Truth and beauty.

  • Artistic director, choreographer-director – Liliya Sevastyanova
  • Production designer Natalia Glubokina on a voluntary basis

The company has existed for 40 years, including as a creative research laboratory of dance improvisation. For the last 20 years, the team has been working in an integrated structure, bringing together professional actors, dancers and people with different forms of disability. The author's method of improvisation allows anyone to speak in the language of movement, regardless of the possibilities. And spiritual aspiration helps actors to gather thoughts, feelings and body into a single whole, as a result of which harmonious and "talking" gestures, movements, mise en scene are born. The company is a winner and participant of many international festivals and projects.

Theatre was founded in 1982. Since 2002, it has been working in an integrated structure.
