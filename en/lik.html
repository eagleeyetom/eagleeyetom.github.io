The performance is based on Russian spiritual poetry. Word, music and dance, when combined, give birth to a single artistic language.

history

Educational: training in dance improvisation and other dance techniques. Creative: staging performances. Showing performances in Tashkent and at festivals abroad.

people

Director, artistic director rolled into one. The performances involve: 3 - 6 professional artists (dancers, vocalists), 3 studio students, 10 - 20 young people with disabilities, sometimes 3 - 4 mothers of disabled people. There are 15-20 disabled people in regular educational classes.
