The Dysonans Theatre

Gdańsk

The Pandemic Card Index

25 min.

Performance "The Pandemic Card Index" is inspired by Tadeusz Różewicz's "The Card Index". The drama turned out to be extremely actual. Just like the hero of Różewicz was dealing with the trauma of war, we have faced the pandemics times. The bed became the center of the world, because our hero was ordered a ten-day quarantine. Out of bed he was using a laptop, Internet and phone to connect to the world. Where there is a bed, there is a sleep. Where is a sleep, there is a dream. We show the oneirism of the scenes through music and dance. Acting scenes are interspersed with dance scenes.

  • Katarzyna Kamińska – director
  • Magdalena Przygocka – director, choreographer

The Dysonans Theatre was founded at the 1st and 2nd degree General Education School in Gdańsk in 2014. In 2014 we have prepared our first performance - „Fairy-tale nativity play”. In 2015, we played an original performance - „Mucha flejtucha”, in which acted around 100 people. Another performance is the staging of the second part of "Dziady" by Adam Mickiewicz (2017). In 2018, we played the world-famous fairy tale "New Emperor's robes" by Hans Christian Andersen in the form of a musical. Our last original project is entitled "The Pandemic Card Index" (2022) and is inspired by Tadeusz Różewicz's "The Card Index". Each of our performances prepared by the director's and choreographic duo Katarzyna Kamińska and Magdalena Przygocka. The performances combine word, music and dance, and sometimes singing.

In 2014 theare has begun it's activities. Work on the performance lasted from June 2021, The premiere took place on May 19, 2022.
