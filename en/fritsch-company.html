Fritsch Company

Madrid

BECOMING

57 min.

Relationships and links that explore our innermost self, our childhood, our way of loving, of wanting or being.

BECOMING is born from the company's creative processes, in which dancers explore from expressive and own body languages . Set in different contexts, relationships and personal ties, the pieces are link independently together and make up the show.

BECOMING consists of four pieces: MARE GEO, BUNKER, WALL WITH WALL and 1+1=3

  • Artistic Director: Gabriela Martín
  • Light and scenography: Clemente Jiménez
  • Clothes design: Josep Ahumada
  • Tour coordinator: Clarisa García
  • Dancers and choreographers: Germán Morales, Julen Serrano, Ana Fernández, Yael López, Ana Mª Alonso, Ramón Marcos, Adrian Sáez, Esther Gómez and Adrián López.

Fritsch CO is a professional theatre and dance inclusive company of two casts with a total of 16 selected dancers , with and without diversity. This company seeks for employment integration of people with diversity through performing arts. To promote the normalization of the inclusive scene, and the artistic quality of the company, in 2012 Fritsch Company started the project of ARTISTIC COLLABORATIONS with scene professionals such as Antonio Ruz, Patricia Ruz, Amaya Galeote and Andrés Lima, among others.

Fritsch has five shows on tour: Peerlees, Becoming, Table for Thre3, Programmed Obsolescence, and Love does not last forever.
