The Myth of Birth

31 min.

We all live in at least three former - who we really are, what we imagine ourselves to be, what we want to be. Who we really are is unclear. As we imagine ourselves, we are wrong. What we want to be.

My name is Ernesta Žemaitytė. I am from Lithuania. I have been diagnosed with cerebral palsy since childhood. But this diagnosis did not stop me from becoming one of the first women in Lithuania to create and perform solo pantomime stories for three years. My inspiration and encouragement is famous professional mimicry from Lithuania Virgis Bortkevičius. He has been on stage for 30 years and I have experience, I have been performing with various groups of people with disabilities for 15 years.

For the last three years, I have been creating solo pantomime etudes, which continue to be successfully performed in many countries around the world. I work in projects with various theater and stage professionals.
