Goals of the theatre:

The purpose of the CCROiR is to ensure the creation of a comprehensive system of psychological, medical and pedagogical assistance to persons with psychophysical developmental disabilities (hereinafter referred to as OPFR) and the implementation of educational, including within the framework of providing early comprehensive assistance (hereinafter referred to as RCP) to children under the age of three, correctional - pedagogical, diagnostic, social, methodological, advisory, information and analytical activities, the provision of psychological assistance.

The composition of the theatre participants is constant. It consisted of students of the Central Committee for Regional Development and Development and young people with disabilities attending the day care department for young people with disabilities, volunteers of the city's schools. The theatre actors are 14-30 years old.

Brief description of theatre:

The reason for the creation of the theatre was the desire to unite children with disabilities and young disabled people. The goals was also to provide them with opportunities for creative and personal development. During its existence, the theatre took part in the International festival of special theatres "Nieprzetartego Szlaku" in Brest, Lublin (Poland), became a permanent participant in the international theatre project "Theatre under the open sky" in Stolin, took part in the third all-Ukrainian festival creativity of disabled people "Restless Heart", the international festival for children and young people with disabilities "Valley of Narcissus" (Ukraine), "Eastern Fairy Tale" (Azerbaijan), "Step Towards" (Belarus), "Lilac Swallow" (Belarus), "Begasis" (Lithuania), "Art for All" (Latvia), "Wings" (Ukraine), "Theatrical Labyrinth" (Germany), "Life of Endless Possibilities" (Moscow). Theatre directors have been trained on international projects for directors of special theatres in Belarus, Ukraine, Germany. Theatre "Fresh Wind" is included in the collection-register "Special Theatres of Russia" issue 4: Moscow, 2010.
