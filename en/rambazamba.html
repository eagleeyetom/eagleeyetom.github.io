Die Ratten

90 min.

"(...) If you want to keep peace - protect the creation."
- Benedict XVI

September 21 was announced as the International Day of Peace. The General Assembly of the United Nations recommended all nations to celebrate this day as a day of universal reconciliation and ceased all warfare. This special opportunity - a joint artistic and social project of disabled people, artists and academic youth - the mysterion - "Day of Peace", we pay attention to one of the most sensitive social groups - disabled people.
