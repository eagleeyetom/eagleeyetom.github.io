Dear viewers,

We have a great pleasure to invite you to Sopot for the Masska theatrical overeview, organized by the Stowarzyszenie Na Drodze Ekspresji, which will take place on November 15 - 18, 2021 at the Atelier Theater. It will also be hosted in the digital world on the website you are visiting right now between 15-30 November 2021.

Masska gives the possibility of meeting artists with disabilities, watching the performances of independent groups depicting different forms of theatrical arts. We invite you to the jubilee 20th edition of the Masska during which theaters from across the Europe will perform.

Masska is an open cultural project in which artists have a chance to show their work. The line-up involves people with disabilities, as well as youth and independent theaters. This gives the event an integration character. An overview is also the confrontation of our imaginations about people with disabilities - artists who often function outside the mainstream of social life. This is an invitation to exchange thoughts and experiences, to discuss them and celebrate the presence in the world of theatre. Above all - Masska is a celebration of life!

We are happy to have you here!
