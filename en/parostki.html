Folk amateur theater studio «PAROSTKY»

Kyiv

Happy exiles

29 min.

Everyone wants a place in the sun... There are cramped places in the sun and spacious ones. There are many ways to have your own unique place, but are all means justified?

A story about oneself, about us, about openness and isolation, about ... loneliness, exile, HEART, FAITH, and LOVE.

The performance is based on the ideas and experiences of the students themselves.

history

The team was established in December 2001 to develop and implement innovative methods of rehabilitation through theatrical art.

In our work, we place great emphasis on creating space and exploring forms of creative self-fulfillment for individuals with psychophysical and intellectual development traits. We study the impact of creativity on the rehabilitation processes of these individuals.

This theater is not out of pity but for creativity. Bravo, because there are excellent actors here. “We do not fight nature; we strive to develop it by enhancing self-esteem and the desire for growth.”

In 2009, the "PAROSTKI" Theater-Studio was awarded the title of "Folk Amateur Ensemble."
