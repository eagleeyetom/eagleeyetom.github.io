Miniatury / Miniatures

20 min.

Miniatures is a presentation of short theatrical etudes inspired by the creative work of the participants of the "Theatrical Emergency" group. The group begins its activities in a new lineup, and for some participants, this will be their first-ever performance.

In "Miniatures," the following will perform:

  • Irena Ciechowicz
  • Maja Cyman
  • Bożena Dzięgielewska
  • Anna Jaguszewska
  • Agata Jonko
  • Elwira Kaliniec
  • Dorota Kuprasz
  • Izabela Weclewska-Brymerska
  • Piotr Dziura
  • Sebastian Sadowski
  • Krzysztof Stadnicki
  • Bartłomiej Szczesiak

Premiere on February 17th - Gadzicki Stage, Helena Modrzejewska Theater in Legnica.

history

Personal development group through theater - "THEATRICAL EMERGENCY"

We learn to overcome obstacles, barriers, and solve problems. We support each other.

We develop our resources, skills, inspire each other.

We learn to give and take. Build happy relationships with people.

Accompanied by a friendly atmosphere, freedom of expression, naturalness, and spontaneity. We develop our creativity, utilize our talents. Together, we create performances and happenings.

We perform wherever we are welcome and can convey something important and personal to the audience. We have been to the International Biennale "Therapy and Theater" in Łódź three times, performed in the Gdańsk Archipelago of Culture, in the Psychiatric Hospital in Lubiąż, in Polkowice - we participate in the annual "Sunflower Campaign" regarding mental health, in Lubin.

Our performance "Cold, I Feel Stuffy" directed by Paweł Kamza took part in the 18th Festival of the Presentation of Polish Contemporary Art and received partial production cost reimbursement.
