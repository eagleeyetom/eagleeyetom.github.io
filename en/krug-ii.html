Game of pearls

75 min.

In Italian, barocco means “fanciful, ornate”; in Portuguese, pérola barroca referred to pearls that were not round but fancifully shaped. Theater actors are just such pearls. Their bodies are unusual, and that is their fanciful preciousness!

Each scene of the performance refers the spectator to biblical or mythological plots characteristic of Baroque art and represents one of the most important human feelings: love, suffering, joy, anger, fear, courage...

The choreography is inspired by paintings, sculptures and music of the late Renaissance and early Baroque. Authentic instruments of the time are played on stage: Viola da gamba, theorbo. For the performance of ancient music were invited excellent multi-genre performers, whose names have recently been constantly heard on the musical stages of Moscow and St. Petersburg: Alisa Ten, Rust Pozyumsky, Asya Grechishcheva. As a result of the combination of wonderful performers, amazing music, artistic design, incredible dancers under the guidance of the young choreographer Tatiana Konovalova, a mesmerizing, incredibly beautiful and simultaneously ironic action is born.

history

"The integrated theater-studio “Circle II” provides an opportunity for adolescents, young people and adults, both with and without disabilities, to expand their perception capabilities, acquire skills in a variety of creative activities, increase the level of social competence and general cultural level, and ensure theatrical programs at various Russian and international venues.

We strive to create conditions in which people with disabilities can become useful to society and reveal their creative potential in the field of theatrical art, participating in the staging of plays and individual numbers, the creation of concert programs, holidays, and performances.".
