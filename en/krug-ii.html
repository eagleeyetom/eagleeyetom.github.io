Integrated Theatre "Circle II"

Moscow

A man from the other bank

48 min.

This play is a performance about the search for your own goal. The history of a man "out of this world", who once realised that he did not want to live his life. A choice was made, but will he manage to keep fate in his hands or will reach his shore?

The action on stage is constantly changing, the stream of time flows, the texts of the preacher about the vainness of all efforts. Being yourself is not an easy burden, you must conflict with yourself and other people. Will he be lonely or will he find other travelers on his way?

The Integrated Theater "Krug II" exists since 1997. Participants of the study - adolescents, adolescents and adults, both with and without disabilities (intellectual failure, mental illness, somatic diseases, disorders of the musculoskeletal system, genetic diseases, hearing disorders). The studio's actors have extensive experience in various theatrical genres, they performed on many professional and amateur stages, as well as on non-standard theatre platforms in Russia and abroad.

In 2014, the theatre received the National Theaatre Award "Golden Mask"
