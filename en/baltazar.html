The Baltazar Theatre

Budapest

Dandelion

30 min.

A dance-theatre performance The Dandelion, is based on 20th century Hungarian love poems. The play is inspired by a famous 19th century Hungarian impressionist painting and uses music from classics like Debussy, Ravel, Mozart and Bach. Dance is a poetic language, the choreographer and the director tried to find the connection between movements and words.

history

The Baltazar Theatre, founded in January 1998, is the one and only professional theatre company in Hungary whose members are mentally disabled actors and actresses. During the last 26 years the company became significant part of Hungarian theatricals. The Baltazar Theatre breaks new ground by putting actors' disabilities in the background and emphasizing their talent. The way to gain society's acceptance leads through being part of their cultural life. Theatre Baltazar is participatig in Hungarian and European theatre life since several years. These years brought that now actors of the Theatre are respected members of Hungarian public life.
