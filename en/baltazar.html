MÚLÓ RÚZS / FADING ROSES

45 min.

Baltazar's cabaret about the passage of time. The fact that the wisdom born with us is not lost, we just forget more and more as we get older. We have to find the true, pure person in ourselves again!

history

The Baltazar Theatre, founded in January 1998, is the one and only professional theatre company in Hungary whose members are mentally disabled actors and actresses. During the last 25 years the company became significant part of Hungarian theatricals. The Baltazar Theatre breaks new ground by putting actors' disabilities in the background and emphasizing their talent.

The way to gain society's acceptance leads through being part of their cultural life. Theatre Baltazar is participatig in Hungarian and European theatre life since several years. These years brought that now actors of the Theatre are respected members of Hungarian public life.
