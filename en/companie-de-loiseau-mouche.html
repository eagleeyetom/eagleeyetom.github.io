Saturne

45 min.

Saturne is the story of a little boy who would do anything for his mom's love.

Saturne is the story of a little boy who celebrates his 10 years anniversary.

Facing a cold and indifferent mother, Arthur starts to ask questions.

Facing silence, Arthur realises that, sometimes, you need to go away to be able to see what's in front of you. Leave far away to find yourself.

Arthur thinks about fairy tales and how there's always a journey in them. A long journey. A journey that will take him through the Forest of the Past to meet forgotten ancestors who will help him mend the present.
