  2. "Awakening" (text by Gasparas Alexa 2009) mystery;
  3. "Peace day" (texts by disabled poets 2010) mystery;
  4. "Feasts of angels" (texts of disabled poets 2011) mystery;
  5. "Noah's Ark" (director Saulė Degutytė 2014) audiovisual art;
  6. "Immortal love" (librett authors Violeta Šoblinskaitė and Gasparas Aleksa, director Saulė Degutytė 2015) Inter Opera;
  7. "Rospiewane City" (director Saulė Degutytė 2018) mystery;
  8. "Luminous People" (director Vitalij Liubota Kiev Ukraine 2019) mystery;
  9. Geo Opera "Virtuvian Man or Phi ", 2020

Brief description:

A spectacle aims to present the atmosphere and sounds of Vilnius as a multicultural city, Multi-famous, international and open.The basis of the mystery are the texts of authors associated with city - fragments of publications from various historical periods in Polish, Yiddish, Russian, Belarusian and Lithuanian and music reflecting various time periods. Willingly shown audiovisual miniatures showing the daily life of the city. In the mystery, professional actors play together with people with disabilities.
