Vilnius

Day of peace

14 min.

Presenting a theatrical performance - the mystery "Day of Peace." With this performance, we aim to draw attention to the traumatic experiences of war and the phenomenon of disability - "...if you want to protect peace, protect the creation of 'Benedict XVI.'"

history

The musical theater group MTT, which includes individuals with various disabilities along with professional musicians and actors. This group is part of many international festivals in Kyiv, Lviv, Sopot, Lublin, Gołdap, Baku, Berlin, Gulbene, and more. Winner of the GRAND PRIX. The uniqueness of these performances lies in the fact that they can be performed not only by professionals but also by people with disabilities.

Modern music composition techniques are used: (aleatory, sonorics, graphic notation method), contact improvisation, psychodrama methods, action painting, interdisciplinary therapy methods in the creative process. This is an exceptional opportunity to develop the idea of cross-sector cooperation and bring out a new, original, and innovative artistic quality and value of an integrating musical work, as well as enable exceptional individuals to create and perform in a professional artistic environment, thereby increasing accessibility and exceptional education.
