The troupe of the MTT Music Theater

Vilnius

GeoOpera "Vitruvian Man" or "FI"

60 min.

Live sound and action painting performances - GeoOpera "Vitruvian Man" or "FI" will invite viewers to measure the golden ratio in the understanding of beauty.

The author of the idea and the musical action is the composer Snieguolė Dikčiūtė.

Music, art and movement therapy methods are used in the creative process. Integrative music works provide a unique opportunity for cross-sector cooperation in extracting new, original and innovative value of an artistic work. Special composition methods and tools are used - modern music composition techniques characterized by improvisation: aleatorics, sonorics, graphic recording method, modal technique, action painting, elements of modern dance.

The texts of writer and surgeon Gaspar Aleksas are based on geometric and mathematical formulas for measuring body proportions and medical terms. In thirteen scenes-poems, the chemical formulas that make up the human body, the "refrains" are repeated (they will be "sung" by actors and soloists - Rasa Kulytė, Ieva Stašelytė, Evelina Jocytė, Saulius Čėpla, Darius Rakauskas).

Based on sketches of body parts and body systems (blood circulation) and visualization inspired by G. Aleksa's texts, a graphic music score will be created. Geometric and abstract figures, chemical formulas will be painted and sketched using wheelchairs and human bodies - action painting tools (artist, art therapist Audronė Brazauskaitė).

According to the already prepared drawings, the graphic score painted here and now and broadcast on the wall of the hangar (visual artist Kęstutis Urbonas) will become a map for the musical score improvised during the performance, which will be performed by the group of talented young musicians "Brave noises" - Mantvydas Pranulis, Mindaugas Stamburas, Dominykas Snarskis.

The unique piece will be performed at the Palukni airfield and in a flying glider, which will be piloted by the famous actor, performance director Sakalas Oždavinys (video)

The plasticity of the movement was entrusted to the master of pantomime Virgis Bortkevičiis, who embodied the role of Vitruvius.

The unique spectacle of action painting will be filmed and broadcast on the screen and NMKC and LŽNS on the integrative art platform, on social networks facebook, instagram and on the VOXART contemporary art platform.

history

MTT unites professional actors, professional musicians and a very sensitive group of society - people with disabilities. We have participated in International Festivals in Latvia, Poland, Ukraine and Belarus, where we have won awards and recognition.
