Teatr Razem / The Together Theater

Gdańsk

Dulce Vitium

20 min.

The performance "Dulce Vitium" is a story about the boundaries of strangeness and normality. We get to know several worlds simultaneously. The world of the stage spectacle with incredible personalities, one with a large head, another with fused legs, yet another without arms and legs. These outsiders entertain the audience every evening with their circus art. But when the stage lights go out, they transform into ordinary people who feel, desire, fear, dream, and love. Teatr Razem, a group of individuals with intellectual disabilities, visits between these worlds. What is their attitude toward human oddity? Who are they in this circus?

  • Screenplay and direction: Dorota Bielska
  • Set design and animations: Jarek Rebeliński
  • Music: Jarek Marciszewski and Remont Pomp
  • Starring: Jarek Rebeliński - director of Teatr Razem, Marysia Krzempek, Gosia Błaszczak, Agatka Prud, Sylwia Zarembska, Jarek Fryckowski, Michał Milka, Michał Tyburski, Łukasz Gawlik, Wojtek Leszczuk
history

Teatr Razem was founded by Jarek Rebeliński in 2004 as part of the cooperation between PSONI (Polish Association for People with Intellectual Disabilities) and GAK (Gdańsk Archipelago of Culture), and its ensemble is made up of artists with intellectual disabilities. For over a dozen years, the theater resided in GAK. During this time, Teatr Razem realized several premieres and performed at many festivals for people with disabilities (including the International Biennale "Theater and Therapy" in Łódź, FETA Gdańsk, International Artistic Meetings in Elbląg). In 2019, Teatr Razem was invited to collaborate with the Director of Teatr Miniatura on a project that brought together actors and high school youth from the Tricity area. Unfortunately, due to the pandemic, the premiere of the performance did not take place. Material from the rehearsals can be seen on YouTube here.

Since January 2022, Teatr Razem has been a resident of the Gdańsk Shakespeare Theater, where it holds regular meetings and rehearsals. On October 1, 2022, the premiere of the play "You Approach My Solitude" took place, based on William Shakespeare's "Romeo and Juliet." (review of the performance by Ł. Rudziński).

Currently, we are working on a new premiere at GTS, "Who's Afraid of Wild Creatures." Premiere on December 2, 2023.
